# Brand

Source: Brand Book — Genuine Gossip v0.1 (May 2026).

## Name

`genuine gossip.` — lowercase, italic on `gossip`, period included.
The period is part of the mark.

**Why the name works.** A wink to *Bridgerton*'s fourth-season catch-
phrase (Lady Whistledown's voice) — recognizable to fans, neutral to
everyone else. Gossip implies trust and intimacy; *genuine* rules out
the cheap kind. Cleared: no App Store / Play / USPTO collisions, no
Netflix/Shondaland IP exposure (the phrase is descriptive, not
protected). Domain `genuinegossip.app` available.

## Wordmark

Set in **Cormorant Garamond Light (300)**, lowercase, period included.
The italic carries `gossip`; the roman carries `genuine`. Both belong.

Variants:

| Variant | Use |
|---|---|
| Primary lockup (light + dark) | `genuine gossip.` on a single line |
| Stacked | `genuine` over italic `gossip.` (two lines) |
| Monogram | italic `gg` only |
| With tagline | full lockup + small mono caps tagline beneath |

Tagline: `CONVERSATION, BETWEEN US` — Inter Tight, 10–12px, +1.6–2.4
letter-spacing, uppercase, ink-soft.

### Construction

- Clear space `x` = the cap-height of the lowercase `g`. Nothing
  crops the ascender of `g` or the period after `gossip`.
- Minimum sizes:
  - Print: 10mm wide (full lockup).
  - Screen: 120px wide (full lockup) · 24px (monogram).
  - Favicon: 32px monogram only.

### Don'ts

Don't capitalize. Don't drop the period. Don't substitute the italic.
Don't add ornament between the words. Don't outline. Don't drop-shadow.

## Monogram (`gg`)

The italic ligature is the everyday mark — survives at favicon size,
sits cleanly on a tinted square, doesn't tip into pastiche.

The **wax seal** variant (claret circle with `gg` in italic, optional
hairline ring) is reserved for letterhead, the changelog, and the
holding-page hero — a gentler regency wink. Use rarely.

Five explored forms exist (italic gg, single g., stacked Gg, seal,
ligature). Form A (italic gg ligature) is the everyday mark. Form D
(seal) is held in reserve.

## Tone postures

Same wordmark, two postures.

**A · Read straight.** In-app, App Store screenshots, newsletter
body — anywhere a sentence does the work. Wordmark + small italic
lede (`Conversation, between us.`).

**B · Lean in lightly.** Holding-page hero, launch announcement,
press kit, the seal favicon. Once or twice — never every surface.
Adds the seal monogram and a regency line (`Dearest reader, ask each
other this.`).

**Rule.** Ship A everywhere; allow B in the holding-page hero and
the launch newsletter only. The wink earns its keep when it's rare.

## Color

Two palettes. The product palette is closed; the brand palette
extends it sparingly for surfaces that need to compete.

### Product · closed

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| `bone` | `#F7F6F3` | Default surface |
| `ink` | `#0E0D0B` | Text, single button |
| `soft` | `#797673` | Hierarchy / secondary text |

### Brand · extension (marketing only)

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| `claret` | `#7A1F2B` | Single brand accent — favicon seal, OG card, press. The "Whistledown" red. |
| `champagne` | `#C9A96B` | Letterhead accent — the "candlelight". |
| `deep ink` | `#1F1410` | OG card background — slightly warmer than ink. |

**Rule.** Keep the product strict — never introduce claret or
champagne inside the app. Use claret as the single brand accent
(favicon, OG, press). Champagne is held in reserve for one piece
of letterhead. Never combine claret and champagne on one surface.

## App icon

Three approved icons. Light is default; dark mirrors the system;
claret seal is the App Store hero.

| Variant | Surface | Mark |
|---|---|---|
| Light · default | bone `#F7F6F3` | italic `gg` in ink |
| Dark · iOS tinted | ink `#0E0D0B` | italic `gg` in bone |
| Store · seal | claret `#7A1F2B` | hairline ring + italic `gg` in bone |

### Construction

- The italic `gg` sits on the **optical** center, not the geometric
  center — nudge up 4% of the canvas.
- Cap-height = 60% of canvas.
- The descender of the second `g` ends at the lower clear-space line.

### Sizes

| Android | 512 (play) · 192 · 144 · 96 · 72 · 48 · 36 |
| Favicon | 180 (apple-touch) · 32 · 16 |
| Corner radius | iOS auto · Android adaptive · favicon 0 |

## Applications

Same kit, different volumes.

### Open-graph card · 1200 × 630

Deep-ink (`#1F1410`) background. Top mono caps row: domain on left,
`PRIVATE EARLY RELEASE` on right. Centered display lockup —
`genuine` in bone, `gossip.` in champagne italic. Bottom italic line:
`Dearest reader — a card to ask each other.`

### Letterhead (launch & changelog)

Bone surface. Left rail: claret seal (60–64px), then mono caps
volume number and date (`GENUINE GOSSIP · VOL. 1, NO. 1` / `MAY 2026`).
Right column: italic salutation (`Dearest reader,`), Cormorant body
copy at 18–22px, italic sign-off (`— the editors`). Whistledown
posture; reserved for releases and changelogs.

### Social avatars + favicon set

`gg` italic mark on circle (or square favicon) at:
120 / 80 / 48 / 32 px. Surfaces: bone (default), ink (dark), claret
(launch). The 32px favicon is monogram only.
